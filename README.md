## Overview

This deliverable explores MRI physics, pulse sequences, and image contrast mechanisms. You will analyze T1/T2 relaxation, explore pulse sequence timing, investigate MRI artifacts, and examine clinical MRI applications.

4. **Submit your work**: Upload your completed notebook to D2L by the due date.
